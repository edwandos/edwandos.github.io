About the band

Birdie Bounce has its roots in the bouncing heart of Ghent, Belgium. This awesome swing band brings to you a rich programme of old dusty and new shiny swing jazz.

Birdie Bounce has a great experience playing their tunes for many dance parties. In the Ghent Lindy Hop scene, this swing band has a fantastic reputation when it comes to setting the swing dance floor on fire! Three steaming horns and a great rhythm section have a bag full of swing classics and their sound ‘swings’ between semi-bigband energy and cosy hot club grooves, spiced with a breeze of New Orleans.

These seven musicians are passionate about the influencing musicians from the swing era, such as Louis Armstrong, Django Reinhardt and Duke Ellington. Once they start performing, faces begin to smile, feet begin to move, arms go up the air and knees begin to bounce!

They are already grandma's favourite swing band. Soon yours as well?

Members:

  • Jan Van Steenbrugge: Drums
  • Sam Opstaele: Guitar
